Signature focus

Imposter syndrome & professional blocks

You've earned your place — yet a voice keeps insisting you haven't. You hold back in the meetings that matter, over-prepare, over-deliver, and quietly discount every success. That voice is not the truth about you. It's a pattern of thinking — and patterns can be changed.

Working at the level of thought, language and identity, I help capable people dismantle the beliefs that keep them playing small:

  • Imposter syndrome and the fear of being "found out"
  • Perfectionism, over-working and procrastination
  • Fear of visibility — speaking up, presenting, self-promotion
  • Confidence at the next level: promotion, leadership, ownership
  • Plateaus you can't seem to think your way past
  • The self-limiting stories written by past experience
