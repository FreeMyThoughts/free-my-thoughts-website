Personal coaching

Become the person your ambitions require

This is deep, one-to-one work for people who are done feeling stuck. Blending coaching with Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP), mindfulness and somatic practice, we work with your thoughts, emotions, body and language together — at the level of identity, where lasting change actually happens.

Where we work

Six territories of growth

Rather than a menu of problems, think of these as the terrain we cover together. Most clients arrive with one and discover the others are connected.

Confidence & self-belief

Dismantle imposter feelings and the inner critic. Build a confidence that is grounded in who you actually are — not in permission from anyone else.

Direction & purpose

When you've outgrown your current chapter but can't yet see the next one. Clarify what matters, what you want, and the path that's genuinely yours.

Stress, overwhelm & resilience

Quieten an overworking mind, create space between thought and reaction, and build the resilience to meet pressure without being consumed by it.

Life transitions

Career change, loss, endings and new beginnings. Steady, unhurried support to process what's happened and step deliberately into what's next.

Relationships & boundaries

Communicate honestly, hold your boundaries with grace, and build relationships — personal and professional — where you can be fully yourself.

Identity & authenticity

Reconnect with your authentic self when life has pulled you away from it — and have the courage to live and work as that person, everywhere.

Coaching is not therapy or a substitute for professional mental health or medical care. If you're struggling to function or are in crisis, please speak to your GP — or call Samaritans free on 116 123, any time.

The engagement

How we work together

01 · Discovery

A complimentary consultation

Thirty minutes, no obligation. We talk about where you are, what you want to change, and whether we're the right fit.

02 · Commitment

A structured programme

A minimum of six sessions, weekly or fortnightly — with support, resources and check-ins between sessions. Change needs momentum.

03 · Transformation

Change that holds

We work at identity level, so the shifts you make aren't techniques you have to remember — they become who you are.
